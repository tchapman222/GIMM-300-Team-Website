About Me
Web development, and technology in general for that matter, has an implied level of responsibility that many don’t remember to consider during it’s creation. While the period of initial experimentation and development for web is at an end, I feel it is up to my generation of developers to ask the “why” regarding the creation of the next level of web content. My main hope for this course is to be able to foster such creative development for future generations.
